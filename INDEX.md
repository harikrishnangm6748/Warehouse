## 13 Components Overview

### Core (4)
1. **Login** - Multi-role authentication
2. **Dashboard** - Stats and quick actions
3. **Notifications** - Notification center
4. **Layout** - Main app shell

### Employee (4)
5. **Create Order** - Add new orders
6. **My Orders** - View your orders
7. **Order Details** - Full order info
8. **Order Timeline** - Status progression

### Warehouse (3)
9. **Orders Queue** - Process orders
10. **Inventory Check** - Manage inventory
11. **Dispatch Order** - Create shipments

### Operations (2)
12. **Shipments Queue** - Monitor shipments
13. **Shipment Details** - Delivery tasks & exceptions
